The Design of The G-Class

Exterior

The exterior of the G-Class

Only the G-Class looks like a G-Class. Timeless design elements such as the front with round headlamps or the clear, angular lines create a unique recognition value. For over 40 years, the G-Class has consistently remained true to its own style. As an icon. Never changed, only constantly improved.

Functional,robust front end

No mistake possible, because there is no second chance to make a first impression. The G-Class, always on its way to new horizons.

Clear lines and large, flat surfaces

Unmistakable from every perspective: clear lines, expansive surfaces, horizontal orientation and short overhangs determine the proportions.

Interior

The G-Class remains true to itself: In the interior, it combines impressive high-value appeal with a strong design idiom. Fine materials and a perfect finish enrich the interior. Details such as the air vents with angular surround and the grab handle for the front passenger are typical design elements of the off-road vehicle.

Widescreen cockpit

Futuristic-looking display concept: two 12.3-inch screens for the instrument display and media display are installed together under a continuous glass cover. This way you can determine the content of the dynamic displays. With 3 display styles you can adjust how information is depicted in the instrument display to suit your preferences.
